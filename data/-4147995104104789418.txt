<a href="http://www.lib.xjtu.edu.cn/" target="_blank" title="进入钱学森图书馆">进入钱学森图书馆</a>
</div>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei">        西安交通大学图书馆的前身为1896年创建于上海的南洋公学藏书楼。1919年10月建成图书馆大楼，命名为交通部上海工业专门学校图书馆。1921年更名为交通大学图书馆。1956年图书馆大部分工作人员及百分之九十二的藏书随交通大学内迁西安。1957年9月，交通大学图书馆随校分设西安、上海两地。1959年9月，交通大学西安部分和上海部分独立建校，图书馆相应定名为西安交通大学图书馆。</span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        原西安交通大学图书馆由两部分组成，北楼建于</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">1961</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">7</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">月，建筑面积为</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">11189</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">平方米，南楼</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">1991</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">3</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">月投入使用，建筑面积为</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">18005</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">平方米。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">1995</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">5</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">4</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">日，经中共中央宣传部批准，将原西安交大图书馆命名为钱学森图书馆，同年江泽民主席为之题写了馆名。</span></span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        2000</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">月，原西安交通大学与原西安医科大学和原陕西财经学院合并组建成新西安交通大学，原三校图书馆随之合并重组，规模进一步扩大，合校后的西安交大图书馆下设钱学森图书馆和</span><img border="0" hspace="0" id="CB9E" src="../__local/8/62/A1/285BEB3A5295165DD3AC9263148_BA8499BC_CB9E.jpg" style="float: right" vspace="0" width="164"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">医学、财经两个分馆。</span></img></span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">2003</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">8</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">月，医学和财经分馆搬入新建的西校区图书科研楼，合并为西校区图书馆。西校区图书馆建筑面积</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">7200</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">平方米。至此，图书馆总建筑面积</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">36394</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">平方米。</span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        截至</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">2018</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">年底，图书馆累计藏书近</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">563</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">万册（件），报刊</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">10089</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">种，现刊</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">1500</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">余种。形成了理、工、管、文相结合，以机械、能源、动力、电气、电子、信息、材料、控制、与医学、财经为重点的多学科藏书体系。近年来，图书馆将电子资源建设放在重要地位，引进国内外电子资源</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">118</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">个平台近</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">320</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">个子库，拥有中外文全文电子期刊8万</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">余种，电子图书超过</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">160</span><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">万种。图书馆还重视收藏反映世界先进科技水平的原版书刊，有</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: <p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        本馆遵循资源共享的原则，广泛为校内外读者服务，除书刊的借阅、参考咨询外，还提供科技查新、查收查引、课题检索、馆际互借、文献传递、读者培训、专题讲座、文印服务等多类型、多层次的服务。本馆是图书馆学硕士学位授权点，还为本科生、研究生开设《信息检索与利用》课程。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        图书馆采用INNOPAC自动化集成系统。图书馆的管理、采访、编目、流通、期刊、OPAC等有关业务都实现了自动化。图书馆自动化集成系统与校园网、CERNET和Internet连接。目前校内外用户均可通过互联网访问图书馆。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        图书馆重视国际学术交流，目前与9个国家和地区的25所大学及学术团体建立书刊资料交换关系。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p class="indent"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 等线"><span style="font-family: 宋体,simsun;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none"><span style="font-family: 微软雅黑,microsoft yahei;font-size: 14px;text-decoration: none">        中国高等教育文献保障系统(CALIS)西北地区文献信息中心和陕西省高校图书情报工作委员会秘书处设在西安交通大学图书馆。图书馆还负责管理国家教育部设在我校、面向全国高校服务的外国教材中心和高等学校科技项目咨询及成果查新工作站。</span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
